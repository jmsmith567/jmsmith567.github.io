Portland Camera Club

About

The Portland Camera Club (Portland, Maine) has welcomed photography enthusiasts of all levels since 1899.

Meetings

Meetings are held on Mondays from September through May at the Woodfords Club, 179 Woodford Street in Portland.

Competitions

Competitions are one of the club's major activities. They provide a way for members to learn and grow as photographers, as well as the opportunity to observe the work of fellow members. Participation in competitions is optional.
