Become A Member

We meet to display and discuss photography in a friendly atmosphere. Visitors are always welcome. Anyone may participate, at no charge, in meetings during September and October. To continue participation in club activities, individuals must pay their annual dues by the end of the first meeting in November.

The Portland Camera Club is a charter member of the Photographic Society of America and the New England Camera Club Council.
