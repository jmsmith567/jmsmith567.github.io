Meetings

We meet to display and discuss photography in a friendly atmosphere. Meetings are held on Mondays from September through May at the Woodfords Club, 179 Woodford Street in Portland.

Club News & Information

Mon Jan 11 - 7 PM
Print Competition (Assigned Subject: Song Title)
Two prints per member. Please note: this competition has an assigned subject so all entries must have been taken after May 18, 2015. The deadline for bringing in your prints is Jan 4th.

Mon Jan 18 - 7 PM
Guest Speaker: Andre Gallant (Executive Board meets at 6 PM)
Expressive photographer Andre Gallant is a popular workshop leader and the author of numerous books, including Photo Impressionism and The Subjective Image (both co-authored with Freeman Patterson). Visit www.andregallant.com.

Membership

Visitors are always welcome. Anyone may participate, at no charge, in meetings during September and October. To continue participation in club activities, individuals must pay annual dues of $50.00 by the end of the first meeting in November.

Assigned Subjects

Three times per year, subjects will be assigned for print and digital competitions. The assigned subjects for 2015-2016 are:

Motion Blur (12/7)
Song Titles (1/11)
Rain, Fog or Atmospheric Conditions (4/4)
